Golconda Fort

Famous for Diamonds & Acoustic Wonders

History

Golconda Fort was built by the Kakatiya dynasty and later expanded by the Qutb Shahi rulers. It was once a famous diamond trading center, known for producing gems like the Koh-i-Noor.

Architecture

The fort is known for its unique acoustic system, massive gates, royal apartments, and underground chambers. A clap at the entrance can be heard at the highest point of the fort.
