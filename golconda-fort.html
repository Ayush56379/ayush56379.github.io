About Golconda Fort

Golconda Fort is located in Hyderabad, Telangana. It is famous for its brilliant acoustic system and historic diamond trade.

History

Originally built in the 12th century by the Kakatiya dynasty, the fort was later expanded by the Qutb Shahi rulers. Golconda was once a major center for diamond trading, including the famous Koh-i-Noor diamond.

Architecture

The fort features massive gates, royal apartments, temples, mosques, and advanced water supply systems. Its acoustic design allows a clap at the entrance to be heard at the top of the fort.

Light & Sound Show

Golconda Fort hosts an evening light and sound show that narrates its glorious history.

Best Time to Visit

October to March is the best time due to pleasant weather.

Entry Fee & Timings

Indian Visitors: ₹25 Foreign Visitors: ₹300 Timings: 9:00 AM – 5:30 PM
