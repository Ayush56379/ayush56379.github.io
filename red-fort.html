Red Fort, Delhi

Iconic Mughal Fort of India

History

The Red Fort (Lal Qila) was built in 1648 by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan. It served as the main residence of the Mughal emperors for nearly 200 years.

Architecture

Constructed with red sandstone, the fort features massive walls, Lahori Gate, Diwan-e-Aam, and Diwan-e-Khas. It represents classic Mughal architecture.
