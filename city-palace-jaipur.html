City Palace Jaipur

Royal Residence of Jaipur Maharaja

History

City Palace was built in 1727 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II. It remains the residence of the royal family of Jaipur.

Architecture

The palace is a beautiful blend of Rajput, Mughal and European architecture. It includes Mubarak Mahal, Chandra Mahal and beautiful courtyards.
