Amber Fort, Jaipur

Back to Home

Amber Fort, also known as Amer Fort, is a majestic palace located in Jaipur, Rajasthan. Built in 1592 by Raja Man Singh, it showcases a blend of Rajput and Mughal architecture.

History

Constructed during the reign of Mughal Emperor Akbar, it served as the capital of the Kachwaha Rajputs. The fort has witnessed numerous battles and royal events.

Architecture

Features red sandstone walls, intricate carvings, and cooling systems. Key attractions include the Sukh Niwas and Ganesh Pol.

How to Reach

Location: 11 km from Jaipur. Ticket: INR 200. Best time: October-March.
