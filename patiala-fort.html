About Patiala Fort

Patiala Fort, popularly known as Qila Mubarak, is a historic Sikh fort located in Punjab. It represents the royal heritage of the Patiala dynasty.

History

The fort was built in 1763 by Baba Ala Singh, the founder of the Patiala dynasty. It served as the main residence of the rulers of Patiala.

Architecture

The fort showcases a blend of Mughal and Rajput architectural styles. Its complex includes royal courtyards, palace buildings, and decorative halls.

Major Attractions

• Darbar Hall • Ran Baas Palace • Jalau Khana and Armoury • Royal Courtyards

Best Time to Visit

October to March is the best time to visit Patiala due to pleasant weather.

Entry Fee & Timings

Entry Fee: Free / Nominal charges (may vary) Timings: 9 AM to 5 PM
