Patiala Fort, Punjab

Back to Home

Patiala Fort, or Qila Mubarak, is a Sikh fort with museum and history.

History

Built in 1762, it was the seat of Patiala rulers.

Architecture

Red sandstone, palaces, and museum with artifacts.

How to Reach

Location: Patiala city. Ticket: INR 20. Best time: October-March.
