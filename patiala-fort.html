Patiala Fort

Historic Qila Mubarak of Punjab

History

Patiala Fort, also known as Qila Mubarak, was built in 1762 by Baba Ala Singh, the founder of the Patiala dynasty. It served as the residence of Patiala rulers and remains an important symbol of Sikh heritage.

Architecture

The fort features traditional Sikh architecture with red sandstone walls, courtyards, palaces and museum sections displaying royal artifacts, weapons and historical items.
