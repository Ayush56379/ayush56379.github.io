Kangra Fort

Ancient Hill Fort of Himachal Pradesh

History

Kangra Fort is one of the oldest forts in India, believed to date back to the Trigarta Kingdom. It has witnessed numerous battles and was ruled by several dynasties including the Mughals and Sikhs.

Architecture

Built strategically on a hilltop, the fort features massive stone walls, ancient temples, gateways and breathtaking mountain views. Its strong defensive design reflects ancient military architecture.
