Chittorgarh Fort, Chittorgarh

Back to Home

Chittorgarh Fort is India's largest fort, built in the 7th century, with victory towers and palaces.

History

Site of heroic battles, including jauhar by Rani Padmini. It has Jain temples and cenotaphs.

Architecture

Huge walls, Kirti Stambh, Vijay Stambh, and palaces like Rana Kumbha Palace.

How to Reach

Location: Chittorgarh town. Ticket: INR 20. Best time: October-March.
