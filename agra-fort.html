Agra Fort

Historic Mughal Fortress in Uttar Pradesh

History

Agra Fort was built by Emperor Akbar in 1565. It served as the main residence of the Mughal emperors until 1638. The fort played a significant role in Indian history and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Architecture

Built with red sandstone, the fort includes Jahangir Mahal, Diwan-i-Aam, Diwan-i-Khas and beautiful courtyards. The design reflects strong Mughal architectural influence.
