Agra Fort, Agra

Agra Fort, built in 1565 by Emperor Akbar, is one of the most important Mughal forts in India. It is located near the Taj Mahal and served as the main residence of the Mughal emperors.

History

Built by Akbar in red sandstone, Agra Fort became the Mughal capital. Shah Jahan was later imprisoned here by his son Aurangzeb.

Architecture

The fort features massive red sandstone walls, marble palaces, Diwan-e-Khas, Diwan-e-Aam, Jahangir Mahal and beautiful Mughal architecture.

How to Reach

Location: Agra, Uttar Pradesh
Ticket: ₹50 (Indian), ₹600 (Foreign)
Best Time to Visit: October to March
