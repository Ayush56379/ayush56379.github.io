About Mehrangarh Fort

Mehrangarh Fort is one of the largest and most magnificent forts in India. Located in Jodhpur, Rajasthan, it stands 400 feet above the city and offers breathtaking views of the famous Blue City.

History

The fort was built in 1459 by Rao Jodha, the founder of Jodhpur. Over the centuries, the fort was expanded by various rulers of the Rathore dynasty. It has witnessed numerous battles and remains a symbol of Rajput bravery.

Architecture

The fort features massive walls, grand gates, and beautifully decorated palaces inside. Important attractions within the fort include:

  • Moti Mahal (Pearl Palace)
  • Phool Mahal (Flower Palace)
  • Sheesh Mahal (Mirror Palace)
  • Mehrangarh Museum

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Mehrangarh Fort is between October and March when the weather in Rajasthan is pleasant.

Entry Fee & Timings

Indian Visitors: ₹100 Foreign Visitors: ₹600 Timings: 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM (Open daily)
