Indian Palaces

Mehrangarh Fort

The Majestic Hill Fort of Jodhpur

Introduction

Mehrangarh Fort is one of the largest and most impressive forts in India. Located in Jodhpur, Rajasthan, the fort stands 400 feet above the city and offers breathtaking panoramic views of the famous Blue City. It represents the bravery, culture, and architectural brilliance of the Rathore dynasty.

History of Mehrangarh Fort

Mehrangarh Fort was founded in 1459 by Rao Jodha, the founder of Jodhpur. The fort has witnessed numerous battles and expansions under different rulers. Its massive walls protected the kingdom from invasions for centuries. Inside the fort, several palaces were built including Moti Mahal, Phool Mahal, and Sheesh Mahal, each reflecting royal luxury and craftsmanship.

Architecture

The fort is built using red sandstone and features massive gates, thick walls, and beautifully carved balconies. The museum inside displays royal artifacts, weapons, paintings, costumes, and palanquins. The intricate lattice windows and detailed carvings showcase traditional Rajput architecture at its finest.

Travel Guide

  • Ticket Price: Approx ₹100 (Indian), ₹600 (Foreigners)
  • Best Time to Visit: October to March
  • Special Attraction: Zipline adventure & sunset view
