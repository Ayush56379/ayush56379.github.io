Mehrangarh Fort, Jodhpur

Back to Home

Mehrangarh Fort is one of India's largest forts, built in 1459 by Rao Jodha in Jodhpur, Rajasthan. It offers stunning views and houses a museum.

History

Founded by the Rathore dynasty, it has been a stronghold during wars and is known for its impregnable walls.

Architecture

Features red sandstone walls, palaces like Moti Mahal, and a museum with weapons, textiles, and palanquins.

How to Reach

Location: Jodhpur city. Ticket: INR 100. Best time: October-March.
