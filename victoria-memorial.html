Victoria Memorial

Iconic Marble Monument in Kolkata

History

Victoria Memorial was built between 1906 and 1921 in memory of Queen Victoria. It stands as a symbol of British-era architecture in India.

Architecture

Constructed from white Makrana marble, the monument blends British and Mughal architectural styles. The grand dome and landscaped gardens add to its beauty.
