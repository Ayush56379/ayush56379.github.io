Gwalior Fort

Historic Hill Fort of Madhya Pradesh

History

Gwalior Fort dates back to the 8th century and has been ruled by several dynasties including the Tomars and Mughals. It is famous for the Man Singh Palace and ancient Jain sculptures.

Architecture

The fort features massive sandstone walls, intricate carvings, temples and palaces. Its hilltop location provides panoramic views of Gwalior city.
