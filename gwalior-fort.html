About Gwalior Fort

Gwalior Fort is one of the most magnificent hill forts in India, located in Madhya Pradesh. It has witnessed many historic battles and is known for its stunning architecture.

History

The fort dates back to the 8th century and has been ruled by several dynasties including the Tomars, Mughals, Marathas, and Scindias.

Architecture

The fort complex includes palaces, temples, and water tanks. The famous Man Singh Palace is known for its beautiful blue tile decorations.

Major Attractions

• Man Singh Palace • Teli Ka Mandir • Sas Bahu Temples • Gujari Mahal Museum

Best Time to Visit

October to March is the ideal time to visit due to pleasant weather.

Entry Fee & Timings

Indian Visitors: ₹40 Foreign Visitors: ₹600 Timings: 8:00 AM – 5:30 PM
