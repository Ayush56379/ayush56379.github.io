Gwalior Fort, Madhya Pradesh

Back to Home

Gwalior Fort, built in the 3rd century, is a hill fort with Man Singh Palace and temples.

History

Ancient stronghold, ruled by various dynasties. It has Jain sculptures.

Architecture

Sandstone walls, palaces, and temples. Offers city views.

How to Reach

Location: Gwalior city. Ticket: INR 75. Best time: October-March.
