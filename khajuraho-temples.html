Khajuraho Temples

UNESCO World Heritage Site of Madhya Pradesh

History

Built between 950 and 1050 AD by the Chandela dynasty, the Khajuraho Temples are famous for their intricate carvings and artistic architecture. Originally, around 85 temples were built; today about 25 remain.

Architecture

The temples are made of sandstone and are known for their detailed sculptures depicting mythology, daily life and divine figures. The Kandariya Mahadeva Temple is the largest and most ornate structure.
