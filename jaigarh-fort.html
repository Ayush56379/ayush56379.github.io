Jaigarh Fort

The Fort of Victory in Jaipur

History

Jaigarh Fort was built in 1726 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II to protect Amber Fort. It served as a military stronghold.

Architecture

The fort is famous for its massive walls, watchtowers and the world’s largest cannon on wheels, Jaivana Cannon.

Travel Guide

Location: Jaipur, Rajasthan
Entry Fee: ₹100
Best Time: October to March
