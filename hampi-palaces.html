Hampi Palaces

Ruins of the Vijayanagara Empire

History

Hampi was the capital of the Vijayanagara Empire in the 14th century. The royal palaces and temples showcase the grandeur of South Indian architecture and culture.

Architecture

Hampi features stone structures, pillared halls, royal enclosures, and the famous Lotus Mahal. The ruins reflect a mix of Hindu and Islamic architectural styles.
