Orchha Fort, Madhya Pradesh

Back to Home

Orchha Fort, built in 1531, has Bundela palaces with Jahangir Mahal and temples.

History

Capital of Bundela kingdom, it has cenotaphs and palaces.

Architecture

Sandstone structures, chhatris, and lake views.

How to Reach

Location: Orchha town. Ticket: INR 100. Best time: October-March.
