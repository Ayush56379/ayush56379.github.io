About Orchha Fort

Orchha Fort is a stunning palace complex located in Madhya Pradesh. Built by the Bundela Rajputs in the 16th century, it is known for its beautiful architecture and historical importance.

History

The fort was established by Raja Rudra Pratap Singh in 1531. It later became famous when Jahangir Mahal was built to honor Mughal Emperor Jahangir.

Architecture

The complex includes Raj Mahal, Jahangir Mahal, and Rai Praveen Mahal. The fort beautifully blends Mughal and Rajput architectural styles.

Major Attractions

• Jahangir Mahal • Raj Mahal • Rai Praveen Mahal • Chaturbhuj Temple

Best Time to Visit

October to March offers pleasant weather for exploring the fort and nearby temples.

Entry Fee & Timings
